## The practical invention

At implementation level, this is a system for turning a Forgejo instance into an AI cognitive operating environment composed of:

* **repo-organs**: repositories with specialized cognitive roles
* **micro-agents**: small detectors, classifiers, validators, critics, and censors
* **K-lines**: remembered activation patterns that wake useful mental states
* **global workspace**: temporary shared focus across agencies
* **settlements**: recorded resolutions of competing proposals and objections
* **Git memory**: versioned traces of thought, action, failure, and learning
* **governance agencies**: explicit authority, privacy, approval, and safety controls
* **local/cloud cognitive economy**: local LLMs for background cognition, cloud LLMs for scarce deep reasoning
* **evolution loop**: spawning, probation, reinforcement, weakening, retirement


That combination is the invention.
