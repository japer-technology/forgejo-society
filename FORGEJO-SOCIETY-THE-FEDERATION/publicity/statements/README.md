# `statements/`

> Official public statements and responses to external events.

This folder holds the canonical record of **public statements** issued
in the project's name. Top-level rules and the statement format live
in [`../06-statements.md`](../06-statements.md). Crisis-specific rules
live in [`../07-crisis.md`](../07-crisis.md).

---

## What belongs here

- **Statement files**, one per statement, named
  `statement-YYYY-MM-DD-short-handle.md`.
- **An index file** (`index.md`), added once the first statement is
  published, listing statements in reverse chronological order with
  one-line summaries.

## What does not belong here

- Announcements of project work (those live in
  [`../announcements/`](../announcements/README.md)).
- Reactive social-media posts that have not been drafted, reviewed,
  and approved through a pull request. Anything published in the
  project's name out-of-band is back-filled here at the earliest
  opportunity.
- Personal opinions of contributors speaking only for themselves.

## Conventions

- **Append-only.** Statements are not silently rewritten after
  publication. Material updates are added as dated follow-up sections
  within the same file.
- **Calm register.** Statements describe; they do not editorialise.
- **Authority.** Routine statements require `act`; crisis statements
  require `govern`. See
  `THE-SOCIETY-OF-REPO/01-governance/authority-registry.md`.

## Status

Scaffold. The first statement is published only when one is genuinely
needed.
