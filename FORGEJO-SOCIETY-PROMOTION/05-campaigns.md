# 05 — Campaigns

> Campaign plans, launch sequences, and editorial calendar.

A campaign is a **bounded sequence of promotional actions** organised
around a single goal — a launch, a milestone, a federation event, a
publication. This document records how campaigns are planned, executed,
and reviewed.

---

## Campaign template

Every campaign captured here follows the same shape:

- **Name** — short handle.
- **Goal** — what success looks like, in one sentence.
- **Audiences** — segments from `02-audiences.md`.
- **Narratives** — narratives from `03-narratives.md` it advances.
- **Channels** — surfaces from `04-channels.md` it uses.
- **Assets** — links into `assets/`, `messaging/`, `talks/`, `press/`,
  `social/`, `web/`.
- **Sequence** — ordered list of actions with dates.
- **Owner** — role accountable for execution.
- **Review** — required approvals per
  `THE-SOCIETY-OF-REPO/01-governance/`.
- **Retrospective** — written after the campaign closes, captured in
  `09-metrics.md`.
