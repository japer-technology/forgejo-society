# 00 — Overview

> What promotion means for Forgejo Society, and the principles that govern it.

This document defines the **purpose, scope, and principles** of the
`FORGEJO-SOCIETY-PROMOTION/` folder. Every other document in this folder
inherits from the principles stated here.

---

## Purpose

The promotion pillar exists to do three things, in this order:

1. **Carry the idea outward.** Make the central thesis — *the forge is the
   mind, the repo is an agency, the society thinks* — legible and
   memorable to people who do not yet know it.
2. **Invite the right collaborators.** Attract maintainers, researchers,
   operators, and critics whose work is well-matched to the project's
   substance and governance.
3. **Hold the project accountable to its own claims.** Public promises are
   a form of constraint. By writing them down here, in the same repository
   as the specification and the runtime, we make it harder to drift.

## Scope

In scope:

- Strategy for *how* the project is described and *to whom*.
- Reusable copy, visual identity, and press materials.
- Plans for talks, posts, and campaigns.
- Style and voice rules for all outward-facing language.

Out of scope:

- Project decisions. Those live in `THE-SOCIETY-OF-REPO/01-governance/` and
  `FORGEJO-SOCIETY-PLAN/`.
- Teaching the project to readers already inside the repo. That is the job
  of `FORGEJO-SOCIETY-INTRODUCTION/`.
- Operational instructions. Those live in `FORGEJO-SOCIETY-SETUP/`.
- Runtime behaviour. That lives in `.forgejo-society/` and
  `REPO/forgejo-intelligence/`.

## Principles

1. **Substance first.** Promotion describes what already exists or is
   genuinely planned. We do not promise capabilities the specification does
   not authorise.
2. **Specifics over slogans.** A precise sentence about a workflow becoming
   an activation beats a generic claim about "AI agents".
3. **Mechanisms over mystique.** The project is interesting because it is
   transparent and auditable. Promotional copy should reflect that, not
   obscure it.
4. **One canonical source.** Every tagline, blurb, fact sheet, and asset
   has a single home in this folder. External surfaces link or quote.
5. **Reviewable like code.** All promotional materials change through pull
   requests, with the same authority levels as the rest of the project.
6. **Federation-aware.** Forgejo Society is one society among many. Promotion
   should make space for sibling societies and inter-repo federation rather
   than positioning the project as a monolith.
7. **Calm register.** No hype. No urgency manufactured for its own sake.
   The project speaks in the voice it wants its readers to think in.
