# Retirement Log

This log records agency retirement events with the rationale for retirement.

Retirement is not deletion. Retired agencies are archived. Their constitutions and contributions to memory are preserved. Their lineage is maintained.

---

## Format

```markdown
## {date} — {agency-id} — Retired

**Agency:** {agency name}
**Status before retirement:** active | probation
**Reason:** {retirement reason}
**Performance summary:** {brief performance summary}
**Lineage:** {any agencies spawned from or merged into this one}
**Archive location:** {path to archived constitution}
**Decision:** {settlement-id}
```
