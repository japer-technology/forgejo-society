# Introspection Protocol

Traceability is not the same as self-knowledge.

The society records what it does not know about its own reasoning.

---

## Required introspection fields

Non-trivial proposals and settlements record:
- confidence
- unknowns
- blind spots
- observability limits
- explanation quality
- model opacity dependencies

---

## Distinctions

| Term | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| **Traceability** | Can we follow the steps and references? |
| **Explainability** | Can the society describe why it made the choice? |
| **Interpretability** | Can humans understand the underlying mechanism well enough to predict failure modes? |

The society should not claim interpretability when it only has traceability.

---

## Blind-spot review trigger

Open a blind-spot review when:
- unexplained failures repeat
- the same observability limit appears in multiple settlements
- opaque model dependencies repeatedly drive high-impact decisions

Blind-spot review is a society-level maintenance action, not a local patch.
